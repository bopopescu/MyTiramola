start       : start splitting immediately, no chain splitting, no resets
start_chain : start splitting immediately, chain split every 500 steps, no resets
half        : start splitting 1/2 into the training, no chain splitting, no resets
half_chain  : start splitting and chain split 1/2 into the training, no resets
end         : start splitting and chain split at the end of the training, no resets
always      : start splitting, reset and chain split at the end of the training, reset and chain split every 500 steps
